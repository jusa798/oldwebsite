This graph may be a bit misleading. Seems like I am a beast with Wu Shang! Not true (He's probably one of my worst legends). I suppose I did well the rounds I played with him, but if I continued to play him I am sure the kill / death ratio would move closer to one or even be lower than one.
Ignoring the outlier, Wu Shang, this damage ratio chart shows one very interesting thing, Xull. If you look at the K/D and Win Ratio charts (0.79 and 0.39 respectively) you will see that I am a terrible Xull. However, Xull's attack power is the highest in the game, which is why I do more damage than I take. Even though I die and lose more, I still do tons of damage! Maybe the key to diamond is learning to play a little more defensively with Xull...
The way ELO works, most people will find themselves hovering around a certain ranking, and as they gradually get better, their average rank will gradually increase. I had two massive jumps in ELO. The first in Silver from 1100 to 1400 after I learned Hattori's specials (win rate of 52%). The next jump was with Teros, when I went from 1450-1650 in practically a day after I realized I liked axe a lot (also a win rate of 52%).
A straightforward graph. Hattori is my most played legend, but Teros is catching up. I also have the black Hattori skin which makes me want to play her more. I'll also play some more Wu Shang which will definitely drive down his K/D and Win ratios.
